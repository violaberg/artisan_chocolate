About Us

Death by Chocolate is a small artisan chocolate business founded on a passion for crafting chocolate with care, patience, and purpose. What began as a love for experimenting with cocoa has grown into a dedication to producing chocolate that celebrates quality over quantity. We create our chocolate in small batches, allowing us to stay closely involved in every stage of the process — from carefully selecting ethically sourced cocoa beans to refining, tempering, and moulding each bar by hand. This hands-on approach ensures consistency, depth of flavour, and a level of craftsmanship that mass-produced chocolate simply cannot achieve. Our focus is on thoughtful indulgence. We believe great chocolate should be savoured, not rushed, and that its flavour should reflect the time and skill invested in its creation. By working on a small scale, we can experiment with textures and flavour combinations while maintaining the highest standards of quality. At Death by Chocolate, we value transparency, sustainability, and respect for the origins of our ingredients. Every bar we produce tells a story of careful sourcing, skilled craftsmanship, and a genuine love for chocolate — from bean to bar.
